Sanjhe Sur

The book "Sanjhe Sur" was distributed to the people by the District Likhari Sabha at Ashoka Senior Secondary School (Boys) , Fatehgarh Sahib , on 10 June,2023

At the book launch of "Sanjhe sur" at "Ashoka S.S.S.School (Boys), Fatehgarh Sahib", the spotlight shone on 15 writers, including me and my poems, such as "ਕੁ੍ੱਝ ਅਲਫਾਜ਼", "ਕਿਹੋ ਜਿਹਾ ਜ਼ਮਾਨਾ" , "ਸਟਾਰ ਪੁੱਤ" , "ਰੁਪਇਆ ਮਿਹਨਤਾਂ ਦਾ" , "ਵਲਵਲੇ" , and " ਪੰਜਾਬ " , made my debut in the publication. Distinguished figures like Mr. Jagjeet Singh (District Language Officer), Mr. Jalour Singh Kheeva (writer), and Mrs. Paramjit kaur (President of ਜ਼ਿਲ੍ਹਾ ਲਿਖਾਰੀ ਸਭਾ,FGS), graced the event, commending the literary brilliance within the pages of the book. The highlight of the evening was the special honor bestowed by Manager Mr. Udam Singh upon me for my outstanding contributions and dedication to the sabha, making it a celebration not just of literature but also of individual excellence.

Hasseya di Cheekh

The book "Hasseya di Cheekh" (Scream of Laughter) was distributed to the people on behalf of the District Likhari Sabha on 16 September, 2023

"ਹਾਸਿਆਂ ਦੀ ਚੀਖ਼" is my latest literary creation, unveiled on 16 September 2023 at Mata Gujri College, Fatehgarh Sahib. Within its pages, I share a profound phase of my life's journey, influenced by the guiding hand of my brother, who introduced me to the art of writing. Supported by my family, I seized the opportunity to complete this work, with special thanks to Mrs. Paramjit Kaur Sirhind for her invaluable assistance. I extend my deepest gratitude to Dr. Kulbir Malik, Dr. Sompal heera and Dr. Phulldeep kaur. The book delves into pivotal moments, showcasing both challenges and triumphs. I am excited to share this heartfelt journey with readers.
